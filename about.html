About Artificial Intelligence(AI)

Origin Of AI

Artificial Intelligence (AI) originated as a formal research field at the Dartmouth Conference in 1956, organized by John McCarthy, Marvin Minsky, Nathaniel Rochester, and Claude Shannon.

It was developed to explore how machines could simulate human intelligence, language, and problem-solving, building upon earlier work in computing and neural networks.

Evolution of AI

The evolution of AI began in the 1950s with symbolic logic, progressed through machine learning's data-driven approach in the 1990s, and exploded in the 2010s with deep learning and Big Data.

. It has moved from rigid, rule-based systems to generative AI models capable of creating content, forecasting a shift towards agentic AI.
